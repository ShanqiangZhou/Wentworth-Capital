Business Divisions

Wentworth Capital operates across three focused divisions: public equities, mobility procurement services, and regulated international trade. Each division is designed to be disciplined, transparent, and long-term oriented.
INVESTMENT

Public Equities

Long-term equity investing with a research-driven process focused on quality businesses, disciplined valuation, and risk-aware construction.

  • Fundamental, bottom-up security selection
  • Concentrated, high-conviction portfolio approach
  • Downside scenario analysis and position sizing
Long-term Fundamental Risk-first

Institutional-grade travel procurement and fulfilment services, supporting corporate travel and mobility needs with structured processes.

  • Transparent documentation and service reporting

Regulated Consumer Goods Trading
