RESEARCH PRINCIPLES

Repeatable, evidence-driven work.

We do not rely on a single model or narrative. Our work is designed to be updated as facts change and tested across scenarios.

  • • Start with business fundamentals, not price action
  • • Define key variables that drive outcomes
  • • Stress-test downside and liquidity
  • • Prefer simple, explainable decision frameworks
  • • Document assumptions and revisit them
