LETTER PRINCIPLES

Write what matters.

We emphasize decision-quality over narrative. Letters are intended to explain our reasoning, identify risks, and describe what has changed in our thinking.

  • • Focus on fundamentals and valuation
  • • Document thesis evolution clearly
  • • Describe downside and risk posture
  • • Avoid prediction and short-term noise
  • • Maintain transparency through cycles
