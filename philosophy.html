ABOUT US

Investment Philosophy

Our approach is built for long-term compounding: fundamental research, disciplined valuation, and a risk-first mindset.

Wentworth Capital invests with a multi-year horizon. We believe the most durable results come from owning high-quality businesses through cycles, rather than attempting to predict short-term market movements.

1. Long-Term Ownership

We focus on businesses with durable competitive advantages, resilient cash generation, and strong stewardship. We prefer management teams that allocate capital prudently and build enduring enterprise value over time.

2. Fundamental Research

Research is the foundation of our conviction. We build an investment case from first principles—industry structure, unit economics, governance quality, and downside scenarios. We continuously test our assumptions as facts evolve.

3. Disciplined Valuation

We pay close attention to price. Even exceptional businesses can be poor investments if purchased at the wrong valuation. We invest when the return profile is attractive across a range of outcomes—not only in the best-case scenario.

4. Risk-First Mindset

Risk management is integrated at every stage. We seek to avoid permanent loss of capital by emphasizing balance-sheet strength, liquidity awareness, and thoughtful position sizing. We are comfortable being patient when opportunities do not compensate us for risk.

What we look for

  • Durable advantages and resilient cash flows
  • High-quality governance and aligned incentives
  • Prudent balance sheets and disciplined capital allocation
  • Clear long-term runway with understandable risks
  • Attractive valuation relative to downside and upside

What we avoid

  • Fragile business models dependent on continuous refinancing
  • Excessive leverage or opaque balance-sheet risks
  • Short-term strategies that rely on favorable market conditions
  • Structural misalignment between owners, management, and stakeholders
CORE PRINCIPLES

Consistency through cycles.

We prioritize durability, downside awareness, and long-term alignment. Our objective is responsible compounding—built on research and discipline rather than prediction.

We prefer a small number of high-conviction positions and accept that patience is often a competitive advantage.
